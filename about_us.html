ABOUT US
Full Stack Road is a collaboration of budding software developers from around the world. The group was originally founded as a group on Facebook comprised of coding enthusiasts seeking to become fullstack developers. In teams of between 2 and 7 members, we started a 180 day training camp in coding. As our coding skills grow, we strive to hone our skills in order to provide a top notch, state of the art product that is both easy to use for our customers, and pleasing to the end user.
